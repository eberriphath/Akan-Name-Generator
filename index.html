Welcome to Akan Name Generator

About This Website;

In Akan culture from Ghana, names are often given based on the day of the week a person is born.
These names carry deep cultural meaning,connecting individuals to their identity,values and heritage.
This website helps you discover your Akan name based on your birth day.
Whether you're exploring your roots or just curious,it's a fun way to learn more about this beautiful West African tradition.

Here are some of the Akan names:

How It Works

User Input - The user provides their date of birth and optionally their gender.
Day of the Week Calculation - The system uses a date algorithm to compute the day of the week (e.g., Monday, Tuesday) for the given birthdate.
Akan Name Assignment - Based on the calculated day and selected gender, the platform retrieves the appropriate Akan name from a predefined dataset. For example:
A male born on a Monday would be assigned Kwadwo,
A female born on a Monday would be assigned Adwoa,
